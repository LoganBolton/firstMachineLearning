another of those flimsy stories coupled with most forgettable musical numbers powell and okeefe as battling publicists are quite forgettable however there are two shining moments hubert castle is the most incredible tightrope walker you will ever see  his drunken walk on the wire has to be the most spectacular piece of balancing ever recorded on film he has to be seen to be believed the other is sophie tucker doing a turn near the end of the film her magnetism her professionalism her sheer talent at being herself  well charisma is not learned  you have it or you dont a great lesson for all wouldbe cabaret artists a sad note wc fields in his last film cameo is completely forgettable
