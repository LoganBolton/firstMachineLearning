a couple of cowpokes help a group of mormons cross some rough country on their way to a new settlement this lowkey western is unusual for ford in that it lacks any big stars johnson gets topbilling but his is basically a supporting role although he and carey work well together dru is given little to do other than provide the love interest the best performance is given by ford favorite bond playing the leader of the mormons in fact this role helped him land a starring role in the longrunning tv western wagon train before his untimely death at age 57 featuring beautiful cinematography ford regarded this as one of his favorite films
