wwii veterans return home and find it hard to adjust to civilian life this superb drama is expertly directed by wyler and beautifully filmed by famed cinematographer toland despite its near threehour length it does not drag for a minute the script by sherwood features very human characters and great dialog andrews has perhaps his best role as a man struggling to make ends meet also good are wright as a lovesick young woman mayo as andrews trampy wife and reallife veteran russell as a man who lost both his hands however top honors go to march and loy as a longmarried couple facing challenges while getting reacquainted with each other
