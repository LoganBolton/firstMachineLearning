the history of the fbi as told from the point of view of agent stewart via flashbacks interwoven with his personal life story stewart and miles as his wife are pretty good as is hamilton as an earnest agent the problem is that the episodic nature of the story makes it difficult to get involved its like watching bits and pieces of a dozen different movies as we get glimpses of a whos who cast of gangsters some of the episodes are too long some too short and some just look out of place stewarts daughters school sequence overall it goes on way too long nevertheless its worth a look for its handsome production values
